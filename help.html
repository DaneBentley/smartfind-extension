Getting Started

How to use SmartFind

  1. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store
  2. Visit any webpage
  3. Press / for AI search or ' for keyword search
  4. Type your search query
  5. Results will highlight instantly on the page

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • / - Open AI search
  • ' - Open keyword search
  • Escape - Close search
  • Enter - Navigate to next result
  • Shift+Enter - Navigate to previous result

Frequently Asked Questions

How many free searches do I get?

You get 100 AI searches per month for free. Keyword search is unlimited.

What happens to my data?

We only process your search queries to provide results. We don't store webpage content or your personal browsing data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does it work on all websites?

SmartFind works on most websites. Some sites with heavy JavaScript or special security may have limitations.

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Click the extension icon and sign in with Google. You'll see upgrade options when you reach your free search limit.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your account dashboard.

The extension isn't working. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  1. Refresh the webpage
  2. Disable and re-enable the extension
  3. Check if the website blocks extensions
  4. Contact us if the problem persists
