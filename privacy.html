Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

SmartFind is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Single Purpose Statement

SmartFind's single purpose is to enhance webpage search functionality by providing AI-powered semantic search capabilities alongside traditional keyword search. The extension replaces the browser's default Ctrl+F functionality with intelligent search that can understand natural language queries and find relevant content on web pages.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Personal Information (Optional)

  • Google Account Information: Email and name when you choose to sign in for cross-device sync
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Stripe for token purchases (we do not store payment details)
  • Account Identifiers: Anonymous user IDs for tracking usage quotas

Search Data

  • Search Queries: Text you enter in the search field, processed temporarily for AI analysis
  • Webpage Content: Text content from the current webpage, sent to AI service only during active searches
  • Search Results: Highlighted text matches, stored temporarily in browser memory

Usage Information

  • Usage Statistics: Number of searches performed, feature usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Extension Settings: Your preferences for search modes and display options
  • Token Usage: Count of AI searches used for billing and quota management

Technical Information

  • Browser Information: Chrome version and extension version for compatibility
  • Error Logs: Technical errors for debugging (no personal data included)
  • Performance Metrics: Search response times and success rates

How We Use Your Data

  • Primary Function: Process search queries and webpage content to provide AI-enhanced search results
  • Account Management: Manage user accounts and token balances for authenticated users
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve search accuracy and performance
  • Customer Support: Respond to user inquiries and technical issues
  • Billing: Track token usage for payment processing

AI Data Processing

Important: Temporary Processing Only

  • Search queries and webpage content are sent to Cerebras AI for processing
  • Data is processed in real-time and is not stored permanently by the AI service
  • Processing occurs only when you actively perform a search
  • No personal information is included in AI processing requests
  • We do not train AI models on your data

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: User data stored with Supabase using enterprise-grade security
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorization for data access
  • Data Minimization: We collect only data necessary for functionality
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security reviews and updates

Third-Party Services

We use these trusted third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: Optional secure authentication (Google's Privacy Policy applies)
  • Cerebras AI: AI processing for search queries (data not stored permanently)
  • Stripe: Secure payment processing (Stripe's Privacy Policy applies)
  • Supabase: Secure database hosting (Supabase's Privacy Policy applies)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • With the third-party services listed above, as necessary for functionality
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
  • In connection with a business transfer (with notice to users)

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Use the extension without creating an account
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions at any time

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Search Queries: Not stored permanently - processed and discarded immediately
  • Webpage Content: Never stored - only processed during active searches
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymized after 12 months
  • Payment Records: Retained per Stripe's policies for legal compliance
  • Error Logs: Deleted after 90 days

Children's Privacy

SmartFind is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete such information and terminate the account.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data according to this privacy policy and applicable data protection laws.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

European Union residents have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access, rectify, or erase personal data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending email notifications for significant changes (if you have an account)
  • Displaying a notification in the extension for major changes
