Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

SmartFind is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Google account information (email, name) when you sign in
  • Payment information for Pro subscriptions (processed securely by Stripe)

Usage Information

  • Search queries you submit for AI processing (temporarily processed, not stored)
  • Usage statistics (number of searches, feature usage) - anonymized
  • Extension settings and preferences

Technical Information

  • Browser type and version (for compatibility)
  • Extension version (for support purposes)
  • Error logs (to improve functionality) - no personal data included

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide AI-powered search functionality
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Communicate with you about updates and support

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure storage with Supabase
  • No storage of webpage content or sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and updates

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services:

  • Google OAuth: For secure authentication
  • OpenAI: For AI search processing
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing
  • Supabase: For secure data storage

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of data collection

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account information: Until you delete your account
  • Search queries: Not stored permanently - only processed for immediate results
  • Usage statistics: Anonymized after 12 months
  • Payment data: Handled by Stripe according to their retention policies

Children's Privacy

SmartFind is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data according to this privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
