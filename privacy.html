Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your data

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

SmartFind is committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

Key Principle: SmartFind is designed with privacy first. We collect minimal data, process searches locally when possible, and never sell your personal information.

Information We Collect

Account Information (Optional)

If you choose to sign in to access AI features:

  • Email address (for account identification)
  • Name (from Google/OAuth provider)
  • Profile picture (from Google/OAuth provider)
  • Authentication tokens (for secure access)

Usage Information

  • Number of AI searches performed (for token counting)
  • Extension settings preferences
  • Token purchase history (for billing)
  • Basic error logs (for debugging, no personal content)

Information We DON'T Collect

  • Your search queries or search content
  • Websites you visit or browse
  • Page content or text you search through
  • Your browsing history
  • Personal files or documents
  • Location data

How We Use Your Information

Service Operation

  • Authenticate your account for AI search features
  • Track token usage for billing purposes
  • Process payments securely through Stripe
  • Provide customer support when requested

Service Improvement

  • Monitor system performance and reliability
  • Debug technical issues (without accessing personal content)
  • Understand usage patterns to improve features
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

What We Never Do

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Use your search queries for advertising
  • Share your data with marketers
  • Track you across other websites
  • Store your search content or page data

AI Search Privacy

How AI Search Works

When you use AI search (queries starting with /), we process your request as follows:

  1. Your search query and relevant page content are sent to our AI service
  2. The AI analyzes the content to find matches for your query
  3. Results are returned to highlight matches on your page
  4. No data is stored permanently - processing is temporary

AI Service Provider

We use Cerebras AI for processing AI search queries. Cerebras processes the data according to their privacy policy and does not store or use the data for training purposes.

Data Handling:

  • Data is processed in real-time and not stored
  • Content is only used to generate search results
  • No personal information is included in AI requests
  • Processing is encrypted and secure

Data Storage & Security

Where Data is Stored

Local Storage: Settings and preferences stored in your browser
Secure Database: Account info and token counts stored on Supabase
Payment Data: Handled securely by Stripe (we don't store payment info)

Security Measures

• All data transmitted over HTTPS encryption
• OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
• Regular security audits and updates
• Minimal data collection principle

Children's Privacy

SmartFind is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:
